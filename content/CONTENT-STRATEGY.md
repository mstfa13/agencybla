## ğŸ¯ Content Philosophy

### Core Principles:
1. **Scannable:** Users should grasp key information in 3-5 seconds
2. **Actionable:** Every section should guide toward next step
3. **Conversational:** Professional yet approachable tone
4. **Benefit-Focused:** Lead with "what's in it for them"
5. **Mobile-Optimized:** Short paragraphs, bullet points, clear headers

### Writing for Mobile:
- **Sentences:** 15-20 words maximum
- **Paragraphs:** 2-4 lines (40-60 words)
- **Headings:** Clear, descriptive, scannable
- **Lists:** Use bullets/numbers for easy scanning
- **CTAs:** Action-oriented, specific, urgent
